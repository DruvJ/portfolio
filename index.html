About Me

A Bit About Me

I got into tech on a whim, but the moment I wrote my first lines of code in college and saw the result come alive — I was hooked. There's something about solving a problem and watching it work that just clicks with me. Tech also runs in my family — my mother is in the same industry, so I grew up around it.

I'm not someone who fits neatly into one box. I'm adaptable — give me a role, a team, and a goal, and I'll learn what I need to deliver. I care about the user experience because I've always been a user first.

Outside of work, you'll find me at the gym, gaming, or on a badminton court. I'm also a bit of a foodie — once I find something I like at a place, I stick with it.
